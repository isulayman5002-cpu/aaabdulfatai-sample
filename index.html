First, The Concept

What is fractional ownership?

Fractional ownership allows multiple investors to co-own a single property by dividing it into smaller investment units, or shares. Each investor buys a portion, earns returns, and shares profit when the property is sold or rented — the same principle as owning shares in a company, applied to a physical house.
